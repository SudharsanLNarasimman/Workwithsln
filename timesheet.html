Simplify and streamline your timesheet process with our modern and intuitive website. Say goodbye to manual errors and inefficiencies and hello to seamless tracking and management of work hours.
Our Timesheet website is a game-changer in the realm of work hour tracking. With a focus on simplicity, accuracy, and efficiency, it eliminates the hassles of manual timesheet processes. Say goodbye to spreadsheet chaos and hello to a streamlined system that empowers employees and managers alike. Problem Statement Many companies struggle with manual and outdated timesheet processes, leading to inefficiencies, errors, and difficulty in tracking work hours and project timelines accurately.
Solution The Timesheet website revolutionizes timesheet management by offering a user-friendly interface with modern features. Key functionalities include a calendar view for easy navigation and scheduling, a day-wise log for detailed time entries, project-specific logging with color-coded categorization for clarity, and user and manager login interfaces for seamless entry and approval workflows.
My Contribution As the UI/UX Designer, I played a pivotal role in enhancing the usability and functionality of the Timesheet tool. I conducted in-depth user research to understand pain points and user needs, developed wireframes and user flows to optimize the interface, designed intuitive UI elements for easy navigation and data input, and created prototypes to visualize and test the user experience. My designs focused on improving efficiency, reducing errors, and enhancing user satisfaction with the timesheet tool.
User Flow The user flow for the Timesheet website outlines the step-by-step process users follow from logging in to submitting timesheets, project-wise logging, and manager approval.
Wireframes - Low Fidelity Low-fidelity wireframes provide a basic layout of the website's structure, focusing on content placement and functionality.
Wireframes - High Fidelity High-fidelity wireframes add detailed design elements, such as color schemes, typography, and interactive elements.
UI Screens The UI screens showcase the finalized visual design of the Timesheet website, including the calendar view, day-wise log, project categorization, login interfaces, and approval workflows.
