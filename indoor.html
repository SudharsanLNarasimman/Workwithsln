Indoor Navigation
OVERVIEW Navigate Indoor with Ease
Our innovative mobile app and desktop dashboard ensure hassle-free navigation within hospital premises. Experience real-time guidance, location tracking, and comprehensive analytics for improved patient and visitor experiences.
Navigating hospital premises has never been easier thanks to our Hospital Indoor Navigation app and desktop dashboard. Designed to enhance the patient and visitor experience, our app provides real-time guidance, location tracking, and interactive maps. No more getting lost in hospital corridors; our app ensures seamless navigation from point A to point B. With features like QR code scanning, AR-based guidance, and comprehensive analytics, hospitals can improve efficiency, reduce wait times, and enhance overall satisfaction for patients and visitors.
ROLE UX/UI Designer
UX Design, Design Thinking, User Flows, Wireframing, Prototyping, UI Design, Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

Feb 2024
Problem Statement Navigating hospital premises can be challenging for patients and visitors, leading to confusion, delays, and potential frustrations.
Solution The Hospital Indoor Navigation app offers a revolutionary solution by integrating QR codes, augmented reality (AR) technology, and Bluetooth beacons for precise indoor navigation. Patients and visitors can scan QR codes at designated locations, receive real-time directions through AR anchor points overlaid on their smartphone cameras, and navigate seamlessly to their desired destinations within the hospital.
My Contribution As the UI/UX Designer, I spearheaded the design of an intuitive and user-friendly navigation experience for hospital visitors. I conducted extensive user testing and feedback sessions to understand user preferences and pain points, created wireframes and user flows to map out the navigation process, designed visually engaging UI elements for the app interface, and collaborated with developers to implement AR features and Bluetooth integration seamlessly. My designs focused on enhancing clarity, ease of use, and accessibility, improving the overall hospital experience for patients and visitors using the Indoor Navigation app.
Mobile App Wireframes Wireframes for the mobile app highlight the navigation flow, AR anchor point integration, QR code scanning functionality, real-time location tracking, and destination guidance features.
UI Screens (Mobile App) UI screens for the mobile app showcase the visual design elements, including the camera-based AR navigation interface, QR code scanning screens, destination selection, and real-time location updates.
Prototype (Mobile App) The mobile app prototype allows users to experience the indoor navigation features, including AR-based guidance, real-time location tracking, and seamless transition between screens.
